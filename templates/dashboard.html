Rehab Routes

Knowledge Sessions

Gain insight and knowledge about addiction, recovery strategies, and relapse prevention to support your journey to sobriety.

Mindfulness-Based Relapse Prevention (MBRP)

Cultivate mindfulness skills to prevent relapse by fostering awareness and acceptance of cravings and triggers.

Group Therapy

Connect with others facing similar challenges in a supportive group setting.

Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT)

Explore techniques to change negative patterns of thinking and behavior.

Art Therapy

Express yourself through creative activities to explore emotions and experiences.

Music Therapy

Use music to promote relaxation, self-expression, and emotional healing.
